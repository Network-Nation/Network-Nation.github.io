About Us

There are millions of independent utility networks around the world still running on spreadsheets, clipboards and countless manual hours. We are building the technology platform to change that, bringing these networks into the digital age. From farm cooperatives in Zambia to entire cities in Kenya, our platform helps network owners monitor consumption in real time, bill accurately, give customers a modern digital experience and integrate clean energy sources and services.


At Network Nation, we take a contrarian view. While the energy tech sector chases big utilities in developed markets, we focus on the overlooked and underserved: independent networks and emerging markets that no one else is helping. We believe in building simple, no-nonsense technology to solve real problems. By doing so, we are digitising infrastructure and enabling clean energy for millions of families and businesses worldwide.

The Opportunity

We're looking for a Senior Frontend React Developer to build multiple customer-facing applications from the ground up. This isn't about maintaining legacy code — you'll be architecting and shipping new products that directly impact how utility networks serve their customers and how landlords manage their properties.


You'll be building several interconnected applications: a consumer app for users to monitor their utility usage and pay bills, a landlord dashboard for portfolio-wide property management, billing and payment interfaces, and real-time data visualizations from our proprietary metering hardware. Each app presents unique technical challenges and opportunities to make a real difference.
